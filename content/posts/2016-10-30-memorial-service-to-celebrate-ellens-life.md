A memorial service in celebration of Ellen's life will be held at the Quaker meetinghouse in Old Chatham, NY, on Saturday, November 12, at 11:00 a.m. with a reception to follow. Directions may be found on the website of Old Chatham Quaker Meeting <a href="http://www.oldchathamquakers.org/directions.html">here</a>. If you plan to attend from out of town and need overnight accommodations, please email <a href="mailto:memorial@harris-braun.com">memorial@harris-braun.com</a>.

We hold deep gratitude for all your love, prayers, chocolate, healthy food, and other support that made it possible for Ellen to spend her final months at home surrounded by loved ones.

We welcome your Ellen stories and memories here on this blog or by snail mail to Eric at 229 Bradley’s Crossing Road, Stop 19, East Chatham, NY 12060.
